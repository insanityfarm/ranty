Comparison

Comparison predicates.

NameCall FormSummary
eqReturns whether two values are equal.
neqReturns whether two values are not equal.
gtReturns whether the left value is greater than the right value.
ltReturns whether the left value is less than the right value.
geReturns whether the left value is greater than or equal to the right value.
leReturns whether the left value is less than or equal to the right value.
